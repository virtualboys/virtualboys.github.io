Whack an Ol' (Blue)



Trapped in a fever dream arcade, Eel Hamburger must fend off his tormenter, a king angel fish named Old Blue who is out for blood. A reimagining of real life events from the show Fishcenter Live.

Play Online.


Whack An Ol' was made for Adult Swim's website based on the events of the livestream show Fishcenter Live. The objective is to thwart attacking "Old Blues" with your eel bod. I was in charge of the procedural eel body and gameplay balancing. Made in Unity3D and playable in the browser, no download necessary.
